Welcome!

I'm Suin Lee, an M.Sc. candidate in Electrical Engineering at KAIST. My research interests include 3D computer vision, 3D generative models, diffusion models, and motion generation. I am currently working on my thesis, "Customized Text-aligned Texturing via Effective Resampling".

Research

Video Understanding Frameworks

Developed a video understanding framework for detecting dangerous situations using AI, including speech recognition and behavior prediction.

Text-Guided 3D Animatable Avatar Generation

Worked on a text-guided motion generation pipeline for 3D avatars, delivered to LG Electronics for metaverse applications.
