About Me

I received my Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from KAIST, where I was advised by Prof. Dae-shik Kim. Prior to that, I earned my Bachelor's degree in Electronics (Primary) and Electrical (Double) Engineering from Inha University in 2023.

My research interests lie in machine learning and 3D computer vision. Specifically, I am broadly interested in 3D generative models, 3D reconstruction, motion generation, and 3D editing.
